### Google Drive Data (Restricted Scope)
The app requests access to your **Google Drive** App Folder (`https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.appdata`). This permission is used **solely** for the purpose of:
1.  **Backing up** your cricket match scoring data (JSON files) to a secure, hidden folder in your own Google Drive.
2.  **Restoring** your previously backed-up match data to the app.

The app **does not** access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive. The data stored in the App Folder is only accessible by the Cric Score app and cannot be seen by other apps or the user through the standard Drive UI.
